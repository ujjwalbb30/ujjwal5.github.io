Hackathons

ANALYTICS VIDHYA - JANATAHACK INDEPENDENCE DAY 2020 ML CHALLENGE :

Problem Statement - Topic Modeling for Research Articles : Given the abstract and title for a set of research articles, predicted the topics for each article included in the test set. Researchers have access to large online archives of scientific articles. As a consequence, finding relevant articles has become more difficult. Tagging or topic modelling provides a way to give token of identification to research articles which facilitates recommendation and search process. It was a multi-class multi-label classification problem because a research article can possibly have more than 1 topic. The research article abstracts and titles were sourced from the following 6 topics: (1) Computer Science, (2) Physics, (3) Mathematics, (4) Statistics, (5) Quantitative Biology, (6) Quantitative Finance. The Evaluation Metric was micro F1 score. The best result achieved was 0.8241258481.

Portable Mechanical Charger - Major Project

Designed and fabricated a portable human powered mechanical charger along with 3 team members. Used 3 pairs of 69 teeth and 23 teeth Spur gears to produce a compound gear train with the final ratio of 1:27 to give motion to dynamo. When applied sufficient force, we were able to generate 12V output for charging with the help of a diode bridge rectifier.
